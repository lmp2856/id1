Portfolio Analysis

Wednesday, February 22, 2017

Bethany Heck

Desktop

  1. What adjectives would you use to describe Bethany’s portfolio?

    Neat, clean, simple

  2. Describe the design of the navigation. What pages are on Bethany’s portfolio?

    The navigation on the About Me page is simple. It consists of three elements located in the upper right hand corner: About, Work, Muses.

  3. Describe the design of the introductory information. What have you learned about Bethany?

    She grabs the viewer with a very large, and witty, header. She follows up the header with a pull quote of her own that also displays a large font size. In her own pullquote she describes her college education from Auburn University and her work that has been featured in various big name magazines.

  4. Describe the design & layout of the body content.

    The body content is shown in three columns. The text based content is separated with two headers. The body content is on the small sifde; a vast contrast to the extra large introductory information. She also includes an email contact box and a downloadable copy of her resume.

  5. Describe the design of the footer. What information does Bethany include here?

    The footer consists of various icons representing different links that include her social media links, a link to another designer, and a copyright stamp. All three groups are neatly separated and organized.

Mobile

  1. How does the navigation change?

    The three navigation links (About Me, Work, and Muses) have now been hidden but are now accessible by a navigation bar. When you click on the navigation bar icon (the three bars) in the upper right corner, the three links will appear.

  2. How does the size of the headline text change?

    The headline text has been scaled down dramatically, however, it is still larger than the body text and still shows a contrast.

  3. How does the size of the body text change?

    The body text seems to be the same as far as font size and style. Instead of being in three columns, the text is now listed as one column, but it still separated by headers.

  4. How does the layout of the content change?

    The layout of the content is now single column instead of three column.

  5. How does the layout of the footer change?

    Instead of the three groups being separated across a single line, they are now stacked on top of each, but are still distinguishingbly separated.

Olivia Tytherleigh

Desktop

  1. What adjectives would you use to describe Olivia’s portfolio?

    Vertical, visual, lengthy.

  2. Describe the layout & design of the introductory section. What have you learned about Olivia from this introduction?

    The introduction is simple and the text is shown in two columns. She lists her occupation as a digital designer and front end developer in the first column. Her second column of text is vague, but she mentions her preference with working on UI/UX and her recent involvment with Sennep & Burbuerry.

  3. Describe the layout & design of the portfolio entries. How does Olivia indicate that you’re about to see a new portfolio entries? How does Olivia lay out the project description? How does Olivia lay out the project images?

    She separates the portfolio entries with a large image that stretches across the entire browser. Each description is shown in the same two column layout as her introduction. Her images are also arranged in a two column layout, aligned to the same width as the project description tet.

  4. Describe the layout & design of the footer. What information Olivia include here?

    The footer consists of five social media and contact links. This is the only layout element that is not in a two column layout and is oddly not centered like the rest of the layout, but instead is aligned left.

Mobile

  1. How does the layout of the introductory section change?

    The two column layout is now a single column layout. The font size is the same.

  2. How does the layout of the portfolio entries change? How does Olivia indicate that you’re about to see a new portfolio entries? How does Olivia lay out the project description? How does Olivia lay out the project images?

    The layouts are no longer separated by large images, but are now separated by background color. The two column layout for the text is now singular. The images are now displayed in a single column, as opposed to two columns.
