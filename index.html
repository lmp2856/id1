About Me

When I first started college, I really had no idea what I wanted to major in. I went from theater to psychology, and even contemplated zoology. Finally, I realized I could obtain a degree combining two interests and hobbies I enjoyed: art and technology.

When I first started graphic design, I wasn't sure how well I would be at using various computer programs to create artwork. Once I began my first graphics class, I realized I had a knack for this line of work. I fell in love with designing and it has become an enjoyable line of work for me.

After graduating with my Associate's in Graphic Design, I hope to either continue school and obtain a bachelor's degree or obtain a job designing websites. I want to use my newly found coding skills and graphic design skills to create websites and advertisements for organziations with a cause. I want to educate the masses and reach out to people via the internet. I want to promote various social causes such as animal rights, women's rights, and enviromental preservation.
